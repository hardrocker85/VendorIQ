A Note from the Creator

Hey there,

My name is Yash, and I’m a 14-year-old student building a small project to solve a real problem I see every day—helping street vendors make better pricing decisions.

Most vendors rely on guesswork, experience, or copying nearby prices. But with bargaining, changing costs, and unpredictable demand, it’s not that simple. A small pricing mistake can mean losing profit for the whole day. I wanted to build something that actually helps them make smarter decisions, without making things complicated.

So I created a tool where vendors can just enter their daily sales—how much they earned, how much their goods cost, and how many items they sold. Based on this, the app analyzes patterns over a few days and suggests a better price to try next.

I’m still improving it—making the UI better, increasing accuracy, and turning it into a full website with accounts and multiple products.

If this idea grows and starts helping real people earn even a little more consistently, that’s all I’m aiming for.

– Yash
